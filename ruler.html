Quick Reading Tips

  • Find the longest lines for full inches.
  • Look for the number next to each inch.
  • Notice the halfway mark between inches.
  • Count smaller marks for fractions.
  • For 1/16th marks, count each small line (1/16, 2/16=1/8, 3/16, etc.).
